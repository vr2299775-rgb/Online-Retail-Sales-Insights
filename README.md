# 📊 Online Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

## Overview

This project is an end-to-end **Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard** built entirely in **Microsoft Excel** using an online retail transaction dataset. The project demonstrates a complete analytics workflow, including data cleaning, transformation, exploratory analysis, dashboard development, and business insight generation.

The objective was to transform raw transactional data into an interactive dashboard that enables users to monitor sales performance, identify trends, and support business decision-making.

> **Note:** The dataset includes data from **2011**, and **December 2011 may be incomplete**. Any decline observed during December should therefore **not be interpreted as a confirmed business slowdown**.

---

# Business Objective

The project aims to answer key business questions such as:

- How much revenue has the business generated?
- How many invoices were processed?
- Which countries contribute the most sales?
- What is the balance between domestic and international sales?
- Which months generate the highest revenue?
- Which products contribute the most to overall sales?
- What business actions can improve future performance?

---

# Dataset Description

The dataset contains online retail transactions including:

- Invoice Number
- Invoice Date
- Customer ID
- Product Description
- Quantity
- Unit Price
- Country
- Revenue (calculated)

The analysis focuses on sales performance, customer activity, product contribution, and geographical distribution.

# Key Business Insights

### 1. Strong Domestic Market

Approximately **90% of revenue** comes from domestic sales, indicating that the business primarily serves its home market while maintaining a smaller international presence.

---

### 2. Seasonal Sales Pattern

Sales are strongest between **September and November**, suggesting increased customer demand during this period. Inventory and marketing efforts can be planned accordingly.

---

### 3. Revenue Concentration

Nearly **47% of total revenue** is generated by only three products:

- DOTCOM POSTAGE
- REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER
- PAPER CRAFT, LITTLE BIRDIE

This highlights a concentration of revenue among a small set of high-performing products.

---

### 4. International Growth Opportunity

Sales span **38 countries**, yet international sales account for only **10%** of total revenue, indicating potential opportunities for expansion outside the domestic market.

---

### 5. December Trend Should Be Interpreted Carefully

Although sales appear to decline in **December 2011**, the available data for that month may be incomplete. As a result, this decrease should **not** be interpreted as evidence of reduced business performance without additional data.

---

# Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory availability ahead of the September–November peak sales period.
- Continue monitoring and promoting the highest-performing products while reducing dependency on a small number of revenue drivers.
- Explore opportunities to grow international sales through targeted marketing and regional expansion.
- Use invoice-level analysis to identify opportunities to increase average order value.
- Continue tracking monthly performance with complete datasets before drawing conclusions about seasonal declines.
